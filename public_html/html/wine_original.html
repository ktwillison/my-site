CME 161: Data Visualization Spring 2016 - Final Project

The Wine Explorer was created by fellow oenophiles Ben Khoo and Kate Willison, so that their blind-tasting companions might have an easier time identifying varietals based on their unique properties. The visualization below leverages crossfilter.js, DC.js and Leaflet, as well as an adaptation of Mike Bostock's Sunburst visualization.
